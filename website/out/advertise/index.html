Frequently Asked Questions

How does targeting work?

Your ad reaches users within a radius of your business location. The base 20-mile radius is included free. You can extend to 30, 40, or 50 miles for an additional fee.

Can I cancel my campaign?

Yes. You can cancel anytime from the app and receive a pro-rated refund for the remaining days.

What content is allowed?

All ads are reviewed for family-friendliness using AI. Businesses must be appropriate for an audience of parents and children.

Can I edit my ad after it goes live?

Yes. You can update your headline, description, image, and CTA from the advertiser dashboard. Changes go through a quick re-review.

How do I track performance?

The advertiser dashboard shows impressions, clicks, and CTR for each campaign, with daily breakdowns.
