## 1. Overview

Play Spotter ("we", "our", or "the app") is a community-driven mobile application designed for adult parents and caregivers to discover family-friendly places and activities.

This app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we learn we have collected such data, we will delete it promptly.(repeat on purpose)   

By using the app, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

**A. Information You Provide:**

- Name (optional)
- Email address
- Submitted content (reviews, photos, listings)
- Advertiser/business information (if applicable)

**B. Location Data (Permission-Based):**

With your explicit permission, we may collect approximate or precise location, ZIP code or city, and manually entered location data. Location data is used to show nearby places, personalize search results, and serve geographically relevant advertisements.

We do not track location in the background unnecessarily, sell precise location data, or use location for unrelated purposes. You can disable location access at any time in your device settings.

**C. Automatically Collected Data:**

- Device type and OS version
- App interactions (screens viewed, taps)
- Session data and basic analytics
- Ad impressions and clicks

## 3. How We Use Information

- Provide and improve app functionality
- Display relevant local results and ads
- Monitor usage and performance
- Detect fraud or abuse
- Process advertiser submissions and payments

## 6. Advertising

Some content is labeled as "Sponsored." Sponsored content is paid advertising from local businesses. We do not endorse or guarantee advertised businesses.

## 7. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

This app is intended for adults (parents and caregivers). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.
